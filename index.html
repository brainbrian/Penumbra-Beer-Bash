Once inside, your taste buds will be overwhelmed by a wide selection of tasty Northwest craft beers, while your ears will be treated to the sounds of talented musicians and DJ's. To top it all off you’ll get 7 tasting tokens and a commemorative tasting cup to cherish until next year’s Penumbra Beer Bash. Remember, this event is 21+.

About

Wtf is Penumbra?

(Definition-a partial shadow, as in an eclipse, between regions of complete shadow and complete illumination) – AKA the darkness that defines the silver lining.

We get sleepy in the winter. It is dark and cold and wet. We see less shows, see less of our friends, and conserve energy to use in those far-distant five days of summer. To hell with all that! Let's drink great local beer, dance to awesome music and celebrate the LAST WEEKEND of Winter in Seattle at a crazy underground events space strategically located right behind Mt. Baker Light rail.
